The Sequels to Dune are a duology of novels co-written by Brian Herbert and Kevin J. Anderson. These sequels take place immediately after Frank Herbert's sixth and final Dune book, Chapterhouse: Dune, and are based on a brief outline he created for an intended Dune 7.
