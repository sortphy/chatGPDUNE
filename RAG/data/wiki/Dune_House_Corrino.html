Dune: House Corrino is the third and final book in the Prelude to Dune trilogy, a prequel to the original Dune series, set a generation before the events of Frank Herbert's original works. The trilogy was co-authored by Frank's son Brian Herbert and science fiction author Kevin J. Anderson.

Synopsis

Duke Leto Atreides sponsors an assault on the occupied planet of Ix to reclaim the land of House Vernius, while his Bene Gesserit concubine Jessica is pregnant with his unborn son. Padishah Emperor Shaddam Corrino IV commences his Great Spice War to create a galactic dependency on his soon-to-be-released synthetic spice amal. Meanwhile, the Bene Gesserit eagerly await the birth of the Kwisatz Haderach's mother by Jessica, but little do they know things aren't going to turn out exactly how they want.
