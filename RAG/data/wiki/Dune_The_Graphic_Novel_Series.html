Dune: The Graphic Novel Series

Dune: The Graphic Novel Series is a comic book series based on the novels of the same name by Frank Herbert. It was written by Brian Herbert and Kevin J. Anderson and illustrated by Raúl Allén, Patricia Martín and Bill Sienkiewicz. It was published in 6 issues, beginning in November 24, 2020 and is ongoing
